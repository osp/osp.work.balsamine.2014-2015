Balsamine 2014-2015
======

The fourth season of the visual identity of the théâtre la Balsamine, Brussels. Filled with engravings, strokes, music scores and animals!

Between score and encyclopedia, the booklet of this season is an essay starting with an interaogation about the pattern.
Inviting us to a come back in the original matrix of pictures, Monica and Fabien woke our moirés fantasm.
We refer to Jacob von Uexküll, the naturalist and biologist point of view.

Linked to its habitants, each 'chant' experiments a specific vision, a treatment.
 
Extracts from the photographies and redesigned using several tools, scripts. Each process interprets the gray values and density ​​of the images to and translate and plot into motifs.

The environment of the page is partitioned and composed in HTML. The program is a long web page, installed on HTML branches, covered with a Less coat

Javascript and attentive to signals. The text currently flowing from one section to another, the images are afloat.

Divided into three parts, the program format texts deploys additional land. Surrounding the central text is fragmented and inviting everyone to recompose its medium, notes, references and sequences associated images.

Typesetting plays a set of fonts generated from the skeleton of the 2 iconic typography of Balsamine identity. The rugged ductus of Ume Plume has been redesigned with autotrace, variations in fat accord with the traced images.
